Help & User Manual

Welcome to Shallion Dementia Support! This guide will help you understand how to use our platform to connect clients with volunteers.

What is Shallion Dementia Support?

Shallion Dementia Support is a platform that connects people who need assistance (Clients) with volunteers who can provide support. The platform helps match clients with suitable volunteers based on location, availability, preferences and required tasks.

Getting Started: Registration & Login

Choose Your Role

Shallion Dementia Support is designed for two types of users:

  • Clients - People who need assistance with daily tasks
  • Volunteers - People who want to provide support and help others

The registration process is different for each role, so choose carefully when you start.

For Clients: How to Register

To sign up as a Client:

  1. On the homepage, click the "Register as Client" button
  2. You'll see the Client Registration form. Fill in your information:
    • Full Name: Enter your first and last name (e.g., "Jane Johnson")
    • Email: Enter your email address (e.g., "jane@example.com"). This will be your username for login
    • Phone Number: Enter your UK phone number (e.g., "01234 567890" or "+44 1234 567890")
  3. Create a strong password:
    • Create Password: Must be at least 8 characters and include at least 3 of the following: uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, special characters (!@#$%^&*)
    • Confirm Password: Re-enter the same password to verify
    • Example: "SecurePass123!" or "MyApp@2024"
  4. Upload your GP Certificate:
    • GP Certificate: Upload a document from your GP (PDF, JPG, or PNG format, max 5MB)
    • This is required for verification purposes
  5. Check the box to accept the "Terms and Conditions"
  6. Click "Create Account" to complete your registration
  7. You'll see a confirmation message: Registration Complete! Your account has been created successfully.
  8. Your account will be activated once our team verifies your email and GP certificate.

For Clients: How to Login

To log in as a Client:

  1. Go to the homepage and click "Client"
  2. Enter your login credentials:
    • Email: The email address you used during registration (e.g., "jane@example.com")
    • Password: The password you created during registration
  3. Click "Login"
  4. If your credentials are correct and your account is active, you'll be logged in and taken to your Dashboard
  5. You'll see a success message: "Logged in successfully!"

For Volunteers: How to Register

To sign up as a Volunteer:

  1. On the homepage, click the "Register as Volunteer" button
  2. You'll see the Volunteer Registration form. Fill in your information:
    • Full Name: Enter your first and last name (e.g., "John Smith")
    • Email: Enter your email address (e.g., "john@example.com"). This will be your username for login
    • Phone Number: Enter your UK phone number (e.g., "01234 567890")
  3. Create a strong password:
    • Create Password: Must be at least 8 characters and include at least 3 of the following: uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, special characters (!@#$%^&*)
    • Confirm Password: Re-enter the same password to verify
    • Example: "MyVolunteer@2024"
  4. PVG Number: Enter your PVG (Protection of Vulnerable Groups) number.
  5. Check the box to accept the "Terms and Conditions"
  6. Click "Register as Volunteer" to complete your registration
  7. You'll see a confirmation message: Registration Complete! Your account has been created successfully.
  8. Your account will be activated once our team verifies your email and PVG number.

For Volunteers: How to Login

To log in as a Volunteer:

  1. Go to the homepage and click "Volunteer"
  2. Enter your login credentials:
    • Email: The email address you used during registration (e.g., "john@example.com")
    • Password: The password you created during registration
  3. Click "Login"
  4. If your credentials are correct and your account is active, you'll be logged in and taken to your Dashboard
  5. You'll see a success message: "Logged in successfully!"

For Both Clients and Volunteers: After Successful Login

Once you're logged in for the first time:

  1. You'll be taken to your personalized Dashboard
  2. You'll be prompted to complete your full profile (address, personal details, emergency contact information)
  3. After completing your profile, you'll set your preferences (languages, availability, tasks, etc.)
  4. Once your profile and preferences are complete, the system can start generating matches
  5. You'll then have full access to all features: viewing matches, messaging, and managing your account

For Clients: Finding and Managing Volunteers

Complete Your Client Profile

To set up your profile:

  1. Click on Dashboard in the main menu
  2. Select "Edit Profile"
  3. Enter your personal information:
    • First Name & Surname: Your full name (e.g., "Sarah" and "Johnson")
    • Gender: Select your gender (Male, Female, Other, or Prefer not to say)
    • Date of Birth: Your date of birth
    • Phone Number: Contact number (e.g., "01234 567890")
  4. Enter your address details:
    • Address Line 1: Main street address (e.g., "42 Oak Street")
    • Address Line 2: Secondary address line (optional, e.g., flat number)
    • City: Your city (e.g., "Aberdeen")
    • Postcode: Your UK postcode (e.g., "AB24 1QQ")
  5. Add emergency contact details:
    • Emergency Contact Name: Name of the person to contact in emergency
    • Emergency Contact Phone: Their phone number
    • Relationship: Their relationship to you (e.g., "Family", "Friend", "Carer")
  6. Add optional information:
    • Profile Photo: Upload a clear photo of yourself
    • Additional Notes: Any information volunteers should know (optional)
  7. Click "Save Profile"

Set Your Preferences

To tell the system what kind of volunteer you need:

  1. From your Dashboard, click "Set Preferences"
  2. Select your language preferences:
    • Preferred Languages: Check all languages you're comfortable with (English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Arabic, Hindi, Russian, Portuguese, or Other)
    • Example: You select English and Polish if you speak both
  3. Select your availability:
    • Preferred Days: Check the days when you need help (Monday through Sunday)
    • Example: You need help on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday
    • Preferred Times: Select time slots that work for you:
      • Morning (8:00 - 11:00)
      • Midday (11:00 - 14:00)
      • Afternoon (14:00 - 17:00)
    • Example: You prefer afternoon visits
  4. Specify the tasks and services you need:
    • Preferred Tasks: Check all tasks you need help with:
    • Meal preparation, Shopping assistance, Housekeeping, Laundry assistance, Gardening, Pet care, Child care, Transport assistance, Technology assistance, or Reading aloud
    • Example: You need help with shopping and meal preparation
  5. Add other preferences:
    • Preferred Gender: Do you have a preference for volunteer gender? (Optional)
    • Preferred Location: Any specific meeting location preferences? (Optional, defaults to your home address)
    • Do you have pets?: Check if you have pets in your home
    • Additional Notes: Any special requirements or preferences (optional)
  6. Click "Save Preferences"

Browse and Request Volunteers

To find suitable volunteers:

  1. Click "Matched Volunteers" in the dashboard or main menu
  2. The system automatically generates a list of the best matching volunteers based on your preferences
  3. You'll see a list sorted by compatibility score (highest matches first)
    • Each volunteer shows their name and compatibility percentage
    • The percentage (0-100%) shows how well they match your needs
    • Scores above 80% are usually excellent matches
  4. Review a volunteer by clicking "View Details" to see:
    • Their complete profile (photo, bio, skills)
    • Their availability (days and times)
    • The compatibility breakdown showing why they match you
  5. If interested in working with them, click "Send Request"
  6. The volunteer will see your request in their "Matched Clients" list and can accept or decline
  7. You'll see the request status update as they respond

Track Your Match Requests

To see the status of your requests:

  1. Click "Matched Volunteers" to view all your requests
  2. Each volunteer's card shows their current status:
    • Pending - Request hasn't been sent yet (no action taken)
    • Suggested - Your request has been sent, waiting for volunteer response
    • Accepted - Volunteer has accepted your request! You can now message them
    • Active - Match is currently active and support is ongoing
  3. Click on any volunteer to view or manage the match

For Volunteers: Setting Up and Accepting Matches

Complete Your Volunteer Profile

To show clients what you can offer:

  1. Click on Dashboard in the main menu
  2. Select "Edit Profile"
  3. Enter your personal information:
    • First Name & Surname: Your full name (e.g., "John" and "Smith")
    • Gender: Select your gender (Male, Female, Other, or Prefer not to say)
    • Date of Birth: Your date of birth
    • Phone Number: Your contact number (optional, e.g., "01234 567890")
  4. Enter your address details:
    • Address Line 1: Main street address (e.g., "15 Garden Lane")
    • Address Line 2: Secondary address line (optional)
    • City: Your city (e.g., "Aberdeen")
    • Postcode: Your UK postcode (e.g., "AB24 1QQ")
  5. Add emergency contact details:
    • Emergency Contact Name: Name of person to contact in emergency
    • Emergency Contact Phone: Their phone number
    • Relationship: Your relationship to them (e.g., "Family", "Friend")
  6. Add professional information:
    • About You (Bio): Tell clients about yourself and why you want to volunteer (e.g., "I have 5 years of experience helping elderly people")
    • Skills & Experience: Describe your relevant skills and experience (e.g., "Trained in first aid, experience with gardening and technology support")
  7. Add optional information:
    • Profile Photo: Upload a clear, professional photo of yourself
  8. Click "Save Profile"

Set Your Availability

To tell clients when you can help:

  1. From your Dashboard, click "Set Preferences"
  2. Select your language capabilities:
    • Preferred Languages: Check all languages you speak (English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Arabic, Hindi, Russian, Portuguese, or Other)
    • Example: You select English and French
  3. Select your availability:
    • Preferred Days: Check all days you're available (Monday through Sunday)
    • Example: You're available Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday
    • Preferred Times: Select time slots you prefer to work:
      • Morning (8:00 - 11:00)
      • Midday (11:00 - 14:00)
      • Afternoon (14:00 - 17:00)
    • Example: You prefer morning and afternoon slots
  4. Specify the tasks and services you can help with:
    • Preferred Tasks: Check all tasks you're willing to help with:
    • Meal preparation, Shopping assistance, Housekeeping, Laundry assistance, Gardening, Pet care, Child care, Transport assistance, Technology assistance, or Reading aloud
    • Example: You can help with gardening, shopping, and technology support
  5. Add other qualifications and information:
    • Preferred Client Gender: Any preference for client gender? (Optional)
    • Can work with pets?: Check if you're comfortable around pets
    • Have transportation?: Check if you have your own vehicle for transport assistance tasks
    • Availability Notes: Any specific restrictions or important information (optional, e.g., "Available with 24 hours notice")
  6. Click "Save Preferences"

Review and Accept Match Requests

When a client sends you a request:

  1. Click "Matched Clients" in the dashboard or main menu
  2. You'll see clients who have sent you match requests, sorted by compatibility score
  3. Each client shows their name and compatibility percentage
    • Click "View Details" to learn about the client
    • Review their profile (photo, additional notes, location)
    • Check their tasks and preferences to see how you can help
    • View the compatibility breakdown to see why they were matched with you
  4. After reviewing, click "Respond" to make a decision
  5. Choose your response:
    • Click "Accept" if you want to work with this client
    • Click "Decline" if you're not the right fit
  6. Once you accept:
    • The match becomes "Accepted"

Track Your Active Matches

To manage your ongoing matches:

  1. Click "Matched Clients"
  2. You'll see all your matches with their current status:
    • Suggested - Client has requested you, waiting for your response
    • Accepted - You've both agreed to work together
    • Active - You're currently providing support
    • Completed - The match has been completed
  3. Click on any match to view details or manage the arrangement

Account Management

Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your account:

  1. From your Dashboard, look for the "Delete Account" option in the menu or settings area
  2. Click "Delete Account"
  3. You'll be taken to a confirmation page
  4. Click "Delete" to confirm
  5. Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone
  6. Your profile, messages, and all account data will be permanently deleted
  7. You'll be logged out after deletion

Tips for Success

For Clients

  • Complete your profile fully - better information = better matches
  • Be specific about tasks and preferences
  • Check the compatibility score - score 80%+ is usually a good match

For Volunteers

  • Provide a clear, recent profile photo
  • Be honest about your skills and experience
  • Set realistic availability - only include times you can commit to
  • Respond to clients quickly
  • Keep clients updated about any changes to your availability

Need More Help?
