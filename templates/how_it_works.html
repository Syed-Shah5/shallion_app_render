How It Works

A simple, step-by-step process to connect you with the right person.

Client Journey

1

Create Your Account

Register as a client with your email and upload a valid GP certificate for review. Your account will be activated once our team verifies your email and GP certificate.

2

Set Up Your Profile & Preferences

Add your personal details, then set your matching preferences — what kind of help you need, availability, location and more.

Your preferences power our matching engine. The more specific you are, the better your matches.
3

Browse Suggestions

Our smart algorithm suggests volunteers that match your preferences. Review their profiles and scores before sending a request.

4

Request a Match

Found someone who fits? Send a match request. The volunteer will review and accept or decline.

5

Start Communicating

Once matched, use the built-in messaging system to coordinate visits, share details, and stay in touch.

6

Rate Your Experience

After the match is completed, leave a rating and feedback covering professionalism, communication, punctuality, and helpfulness.

Volunteer Journey

1

Create Your Account

Register as a volunteer with your email and valid PVG (Protecting Vulnerable Groups) number for review. Your account will be activated once our team verifies your email and PVG number.

2

Complete Your Profile

Add your bio, qualifications and set your availability and service preferences so clients can find you.

3

Receive Match Requests

Clients will send you match requests based on compatibility. Review them in your incoming requests page.

You can accept or decline. Only accepting creates a live match and opens messaging.
4

Communicate with Your Client

Use secure on-platform messaging to coordinate visits, discuss needs and build a working relationship.

5

Build Your Reputation

Receive ratings from clients on professionalism, communication, punctuality and helpfulness. Great ratings lead to more matches.
